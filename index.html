About us

Human interest in animals has a long history, and by observing the appearance of animals, we learned little by little about their origins and ecology. It is believed that humans first used animals for food. Humans have lived in close relationship with the animals and plants in the place where they live in relation to production activities such as food, clothing, shelter, medicine, agriculture, and fishing. As human culture developed, people's knowledge about animals and plants would gradually accumulate and become more precise. In the case of our people, among the relics and relics of prehistoric times found in various places, many relics of living things or relics related to living things have been excavated.
[Source: Encyclopedia of Korean National Culture]
