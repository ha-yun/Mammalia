RABBIT

Rabbits belong to mammals, and there are wild-type and domesticated domestic rabbits. The wild-type wild rabbit in Korea is a common species, and there are domestic rabbits and breeds that have been improved for pets.

Characteristic

The order of mammals has a relatively small size. It has a very short or traced tail, and its forelimbs and hind feet have five toes. There is a pair of large incisors in front of the upper jaw and a pair of very small ones right behind them. The incisors are completely covered with enamel, and there is a wide gap between the incisors and the premolars.

Tale

In Korean folk culture, such as oral traditions passed down from a long time ago, rabbits have been recognized as clever and cute animals. Although it is a common animal around us, the rabbit, an imaginary object pounding a mill in the moon, is a familiar animal and a sacred being.
